TRAVEL BY METRO

From Kuntsevskaya metro station:

Board the first car (from the city center). From glass doors, exit to the left and down by the stairs to the bus stop. Take bus or minibus 575 or 610. Your stop is “Molokozavod”.

TRAVEL BY CAR:

From center: Head for oblast1 down by Mozhajskoe highway. Turn left to Ryabinovaya Street. Go down Ryabinovaya Street straight to business center WEST PLAZA.

From oblast: go up Mozhajskoe highway to the center. Turn right to Ryabinovaya Street to business center WEST PLAZA.
